# Title

## 1. Who built it, and what is it?
- Who built it?
- What is the tool/system/product?
- What does it do in one sentence?
- How does it fit into their life in practice?

## 2. Why they needed it
- What problem were they dealing with?
- Why did this matter enough to build something?

## 3. Why existing tools weren’t enough
- What did they try before?
- What was missing from off-the-shelf tools?
- Why not just use a spreadsheet, Notion, or SaaS product?

## 4. How they built it with AI
- What role did AI play in getting this built?
- What would have been much harder without AI?
- Did AI help with building, thinking, refining, or all three?
- What parts still required human judgment?
- What did the person do themselves, and what did the AI do?

## 5. Challenges they had to overcome
- What was hard about building it?
- What broke?
- What tradeoffs or limitations still exist?

## 6. Closing
- What is the final takeaway?
- Where can readers check out the project?
- If relevant: how can people reach out with similar projects?
